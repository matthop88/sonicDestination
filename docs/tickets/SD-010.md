# SD-010
## Motobugs (Badniks) implemented

Type: STORY

### Description:
Motobugs are placed in the Green Hill Zone, in the exact same places as in Sonic 1 GHZ Act 1.
Motobugs animate as per Sonic 1, with puffs of steam being emitted from rear of badnik.
Badniks pace back and forth, stopping and turning when they reach the edge of a cliff.
Sonic can collide with motobugs, causing him to fall back.
Sonic flashes for an interval after landing from falling back, during which he is invincible.
When Sonic is jumping, he can collide with a motobug and cause it to explode.
Rebound for Sonic is implemented.

### Acceptance Criteria: 
- [ ] Motobugs appear in Green Hill Zone according to exact placement of Sonic 1, GHZ Act 1.
- [ ] Motobugs animate with moving puffs of steam.
- [ ] Motobugs are affected by gravity
- [ ] Motobugs move according to sensor-based path
- [ ] Sonic colliding with motobugs cause him to fall back, with "boom" sound
- [ ] Sonic flashes and is momentarily invincible upon landing
- [ ] Sonic can spin into motobugs and cause them to explode
- [ ] Rebound is implemented
